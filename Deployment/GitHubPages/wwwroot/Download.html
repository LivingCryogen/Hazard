Hazard! is a board game for 2-6 local players.
To install, simply click a Download button and open the file.

Note: You may need to wait a moment for your request to be verified.

Installation Details

Installation may require access to the download source, which is time-limited. If too much time has passed, try redownloading and installing directly after download. Take care: excessive requests may result in rate-limiting or IP bans.

Opening the MSIX package (.msixbundle), you should see:

Publisher Name: "Joshua McKnight"

Source: "livingcryogen.github.io"

In the event you see a warning, please verify this information.

You may also verify the digital signature (see below).


Digital Signature

The files are signed by me, "Joshua McKnight," via Microsoft's Azure Trusted Signing. To verify, and you may right-click the installation bundle, select "Properties" -> "Digital Signatures."
